A shameless fork sorter specifically designed for most (if not all) Manga Time Kirara characters.

Certain options have details that you can hover to read if you're on a laptop.

Click on the character you like better from the two, or tie them if you like them equally or don't know them.

Unless you picked Agony Mode (WIP), in which case, you can't tie them; instead, you can do a coin flip (You will not be informed who won the coin flip).

Depending on how many sources you pick (especially under default settings), you'll get up to 700+ picks, so set aside a good few cups of tea and possibly your revolver for this.



Keyboard controls during sorting: H/LeftArrow (pick left) J/DownArrow (undo) K/UpArrow (tie) L/RightArrow (pick right) S (save progress).

Before sorting: S/Enter (start sorting) L (load progress).

1/2/3 always correspond to the first/second/third buttons.
